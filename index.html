The firm

Light Water Capital is a private investment firm and family office based in Corydon, Indiana. We manage capital for a select number of clients - carefully, deliberately, and with a long horizon.

Private capital management means we invest our own capital alongside our clients. We focus on public equities and quantitative strategies, building concentrated positions where we have high conviction. We don't chase short-term performance or follow consensus. Our edge comes from patience, rigorous research, and a willingness to wait for the right opportunity.

Our approach

How we think about capital allocation

Concentration over diversification

We prefer a small number of deeply researched positions over a broad portfolio. When you know what you own and why, less is more.

Quantitative rigor

Every investment thesis is tested against data. We build and maintain our own research infrastructure, including open-source tools like our July Backtester.

Long time horizons

We underwrite investments over years, not quarters. Patient capital earns the premium that impatient capital leaves on the table.

Aligned incentives

We invest our own capital alongside our clients. Our interests are aligned because we bear the same risk and earn the same returns.

Frequently asked questions

What does Light Water Capital do?

Light Water Capital is a private investment firm and family office. We manage capital for a select number of clients, focusing on public equities and quantitative strategies with a long-term investment horizon.

Where is Light Water Capital based?

We are based in Corydon, Indiana. We operate as a private firm and do not maintain public-facing offices.

What is your investment philosophy?

We build concentrated, high-conviction portfolios backed by quantitative research. We prioritize long time horizons, aligned incentives with our clients, and rigorous data-driven analysis over market consensus or short-term performance chasing.
