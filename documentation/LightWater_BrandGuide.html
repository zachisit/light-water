01 — Brand Essence

The Idea Behind the Brand

Light Water occupies a rare space: the intersection of genuine capability and deliberate restraint. The brand should feel as considered as the advice you give — clear-eyed, unhurried, and built to last.

Brand Tone

Precise, not cold Confident, not boastful Warm, not casual Minimal, not bare Premium, not ostentatious

Highlights

04 — Logo Usage

Logo System

The logo is the anchor of the identity. Use only the supplied file — never recreate, trace, or approximate it. Clear space equals the height of the wordmark on all sides.

On White — Primary
On Navy — White Reversed
On Off-White — Documents
On Pale Blue — Accented Sections

Logo Don'ts

× Never recreate as SVG or illustration × No drop shadows × No distortion or stretching × No color alterations × No placement on photography × Minimum width: 120px digital / 1" print
05 — Voice & Tone

How We Speak

Language should reflect the same deliberateness as the investment process. Every word earns its place. We speak in clear sentences, not jargon.

Write This
"We preserve capital across generations."
"Your portfolio is reviewed quarterly, with updates within five days."
"We have managed private capital since 2009."
"Our fee structure is flat and transparent."
"We take concentrated positions we genuinely believe in."
Not This
"Maximize generational alpha through proven frameworks!"
"Real-time synergistic portfolio optimization, delivered fast."
"Over a decade of disrupting wealth management."
"Best-in-class solutions for your wealth journey."
"We leverage cutting-edge AI to unlock opportunities."

Pull Quote Treatment

"The goal isn't to be the loudest voice in the room.
It's to be the most reliable one."

Light Water Capital

06 — Imagery Direction

Visual Language

No stock photos of handshakes, charts, or people in suits. Photography should evoke clarity, natural depth, and the passage of time — consistent with a long-term perspective.

Natural LandscapesHorizon lines, calm water, open sky. Conveys perspective and patience.
Minimal Data VizClean charts, ample white space, navy on white. No decoration.
Tactile MaterialsLinen, paper, stone. Conveys permanence and care for craft.

Photography Tone

Natural light only Desaturated / cool Generous negative space No people in motion Architectural or landscape Overcast / morning tone

Website Layout Direction

Hero

Full-width navy background. Single serif headline in large display type. Logo top-left. No hero image — let the typography carry it.

Content

Max 760px content width. Line height 1.85. Teal rule above section headings. Ample white space between sections.

Footer

Navy footer. Logo mark placed at reduced size. Navigation in 11px uppercase. Regulatory disclosure in 10px.
